Limitations
(1) No POR failure recovery mechanism is provided.  NetworkTables, sockets, node, and parent information is lost on a power failure unless the application saves and restores them. 
(2) R22 must be moved to R21 for use with Explorer 16 for Revision 4 or earlier of AC163027-4.
